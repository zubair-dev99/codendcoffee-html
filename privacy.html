Introduction to Our Privacy Practices

Welcome to CodeNdCoffee . As a premier software development company, we are committed to protecting your personal and business information while maintaining your right to privacy. This privacy policy outlines our practices for handling client data, project information, and website visitor data. If you have any questions or concerns about our data protection practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer.

When you visit our website, use our software products, or engage our software development services, you trust us with your information. We take this responsibility seriously. This privacy notice explains what information we collect, how we use it for software development projects, and what rights you have regarding your data under applicable laws including GDPR and CCPA.

Section 04

At CodeNdCoffee , we implement robust data security measures to protect your information:

Section 06

Your Data Protection Rights as a Client

Depending on your location (such as the European Economic Area under GDPR, California under CCPA, etc.), you have specific rights under applicable data protection laws. CodeNdCoffee respects these rights, which include:

1
Right to Access & Data Portability

Request copies of your personal data held by us in a structured, machine-readable format for software development records.

2
Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information in our client database.

3
Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions, subject to legal retention requirements for software project documentation.

4
Right to Restrict Processing

Request restriction of processing your personal data in specific circumstances related to your software development engagement.

5
Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or specific legitimate interests related to software services.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@codendcoffee.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by applicable laws.

Section 07

GDPR Compliance for European Clients

For clients in the European Economic Area, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our data processing practices include:

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process data based on contract performance, legitimate interests, or your explicit consent for software development services.

International Data Transfers

When transferring data outside the EEA for software development, we ensure appropriate safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses.

Data Retention

We retain client project data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.
